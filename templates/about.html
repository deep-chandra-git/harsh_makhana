Our Story

Harsh Makhana started with a simple passion: to bring premium, healthy snacking to modern India.

Sourced from Bihar's finest farms, our makhana is roasted to perfection using traditional methods with a gourmet twist. No preservatives, no compromise. Every crunch tells a story of purity, taste, and dedication to quality.

What began as a small dream has now grown into a trusted brand loved by thousands across the country. We are committed to sustainable farming practices and empowering local farmers who grow the finest quality makhana.
